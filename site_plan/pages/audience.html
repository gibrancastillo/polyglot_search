Target Audience

This website is aimed at all kinds of people who love movies, that's why PolyglotSearch allows movie searches through voice recognition, allowing users of all ages to find their favorite movies in their native language. Our application is available for PC, mobile phones, tablets. in order to reach more people and find their ideal movie.
